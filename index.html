FAQ

Common questions.

Do I pay anything upfront?
No. We build your preview site for free. You only pay when you've seen it and want to claim it as your own.
Will I own the website?
100%. We buy your .com.au in your name, host it under your control, and hand over all access. You own the domain and everything on it.
How do you build it without talking to me first?
We use publicly available info — your Google Business Profile, reviews, social media and industry directories. You review and refine before anything goes live.
How quickly can I go live?
Once you claim your site we can have it live on your own .com.au within 48 hours. Usually same day.
What if I don't like it?
No cost, no hard feelings. The preview is completely free. If you want changes before claiming, we're happy to update it.
Do you work outside Melbourne?
Yes — we work with local businesses right across Australia.
